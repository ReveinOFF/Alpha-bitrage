The most frequently discussed questions and topics are presented in this section, so you can easily find an answer to most anything you might be looking for.

1. How do clients earn on our platform?
Clients earn by capitalizing on the price differences of cryptocurrencies across multiple exchanges. Our automated system identifies these discrepancies and executes trades in real-time, allowing users to profit from the arbitrage opportunities without the need for manual intervention. This ensures that clients can consistently and efficiently maximize their returns with minimal effort.
2. How do I start using your platform for automated arbitrage?
To get started, follow our detailed step-by-step guide available here. This guide covers registration, account creation, funding your account, and beginning your first trade on our platform.
3. What are the fees for using your platform?
Our platform charges a 5% commission on the net profit of each arbitrage transaction. There are no additional fees for deposits or withdrawals.
4. How quickly are transactions processed on your platform?
Transactions on our platform are typically processed within 3 hours. This includes the execution of buy and sell orders and the final settlement of funds in your account.
5. What is the minimum deposit required to start trading?
The minimum deposit required to participate in arbitrage on our platform is $25. This price is determined by various factors such as the transaction percentage, potential price fluctuation risks, and our service fee, which consists of 5% of your net profit.
